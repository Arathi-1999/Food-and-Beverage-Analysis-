# Food-and-Beverage-Analysis-

## 📘 Project Overview
**CodeX** is a German beverage company entering the Indian market with its new energy drink. The marketing team aimed to understand consumer behavior, brand perception, and competition in India. This project analyzes survey data from 10,000 respondents across 10 Indian cities to generate actionable insights that can guide marketing, product development, and brand positioning.

## 🎯 Problem Statement
**CodeX** wants to expand its presence in the Indian market. Task is to convert survey data into meaningful insights to help the team improve:

• Brand awareness

• Market share

• Product development

## 🧩 Objectives

**•** Identify key demographic segments who prefer energy drinks.

**•** Understand consumer preferences in ingredients, pricing, and packaging.

**•** Evaluate competitor performance and brand perception.

**•** Determine the most effective marketing channels.

**•** Provide data-driven recommendations for product and marketing strategies.

## 🧠 Key Insights

**1. Demographics**

• Males (60.38%) are the primary consumers.

• The 19–30 age group shows the highest preference (5.5K respondents).

• Online ads reach the most youth (15–30 years).

**2. Consumer Preferences**

• Most preferred ingredient: Caffeine (30%)

• Preferred packaging: Compact and portable cans

**3. Competition**

• Market leaders: Cola-Coka (25.4%), Bepsi (21.1%), Gangster (18.5%)

• Main reasons for preferring competitors: Brand reputation and Availability

**4. Brand Penetration**
• CodeX average rating: 3.27 / 5 (industry average)

• Focus cities: Bangalore, Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Pune.

**5. Purchasing Behavior**

• Most buyers purchase from Supermarkets (44.9%)

• Top usage occasions: Sports/Exercise (45%) and Studying/Working late (32%)

• Preferred price range: ₹50–99
